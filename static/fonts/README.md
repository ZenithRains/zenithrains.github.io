The site bundles its fonts locally and makes no third-party font requests.

- Source Sans 3: `@fontsource-variable/source-sans-3` 5.2.9
- Source Serif 4: `@fontsource-variable/source-serif-4` 5.2.9
- Noto Sans SC: `@fontsource-variable/noto-sans-sc` 5.2.10
- Noto Serif SC: `@fontsource-variable/noto-serif-sc` 5.2.10

The Noto CJK fonts are the Google-branded counterparts of Adobe's Source Han
families. All bundled typefaces are licensed under the SIL Open Font License
1.1; a copy is included in `OFL-1.1.txt`.
